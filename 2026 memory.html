Hanbok Rental · Gyeongbokgung Station Exit 4
韩服租借 · 景福宫站4号出口
Taxi ~25 min from Gangnam (₩25,000). Shops cluster around Exit 4. Recommended: Hanboknam or Daehan Hanbok — 500+ designs incl. hair styling. Wearing Hanbok = FREE palace entry. Dressing + hair ~45–60 min. Rental: 3–4 hours.
从江南打的约25分钟（₩25,000）。韩服店集中在景福宫站4号出口。推荐：Hanboknam或大韩韩服——超500款，含发型。穿韩服入宫免费。换装+发型约45–60分钟，租借3–4小时。
👘 ₩20,000–35,000/人 含发型🚕 ₩25,000 from Gangnam
12:30 PM
경복궁 Gyeongbokgung Palace · Royal Guard Ceremony
景福宫 · 朝鲜王朝主宫 · 换岗仪式
Walk 5 min from Hanboknam to Gwanghwamun Gate. Built 1395. Must-see inside: Geunjeongjeon throne hall, Gyeonghoeru Pavilion on water, Hyangwonjeong Pavilion back garden (most photogenic). Royal Guard Changing Ceremony at 2pm — plan to be at Gwanghwamun Gate by then. ~1.5 hours inside.
从韩服店步行5分钟至光化门。建于1395年。宫内必看：勤政殿（王座殿）、庆会楼（水上阁）、香远亭（后花园最佳拍照）。下午2点换岗仪式——计划好在光化门守候。宫内游览约1.5小时。
🏯 穿韩服免费入场🎖️ 换岗仪式 2pm🚶 5min walk from Hanboknam
2:00 PM
북촌 한옥마을 Bukchon Hanok Village
北村韩屋村 · 600年原味石巷
10–15 min walk east from the palace. Still in Hanbok — perfect. Clay-tiled rooftops and stone alleys unchanged 600 years. Walk Gahoe-ro 11-gil for the iconic uphill alley view. Quiet residential area — keep voices low.
从宫殿向东步行10–15分钟。仍穿韩服——最完美组合。600年未变的瓦顶石巷。走嘉会路11街拍最经典画面。住宅区——请压低音量。
🏘️ Free · ~45min🚶 10–15min walk from Palace

🌉 한강 Han River Picnic · 치맥 Chimaek Night
汉江野餐 · 炸鸡啤酒之夜 · 彩虹喷泉
